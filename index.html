The Artistic Duel

Writing Time
10:00

Duel Rules:

  1. Get assigned your painting team.
  2. Individually: Every student must write their own set of 6 sentences on their part of the table.
  3. Sentence Requirements: 3 with ERA (descriptions) and 3 with ESTABA (feelings/locations).
  4. The Judging: Once the timer hits zero, everyone moves to a neighboring table to "judge" who has the most accurate/creative sentences.

"May the best critic win!"

Art Critics in Transit

Stand up! Find a partner from the opposite team.
